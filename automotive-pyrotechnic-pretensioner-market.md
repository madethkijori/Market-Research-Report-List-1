<p><strong>The Automotive Pyrotechnic Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive pyrotechnic pretensioner market is segmented into two primary types: buckle pretensioners and retractor pretensioners. Buckle pretensioners are integrated into the seatbelt buckle, deploying to tighten the belt during a collision, minimizing slack and enhancing occupant safety. Retractor pretensioners, on the other hand, are located within the seatbelt retractor mechanism. They activate to pull the belt tight against the occupant upon impact, ensuring proper restraint. Both types contribute significantly to the overall effectiveness of passive safety systems in vehicles.</p></p>
