<p><p>The orthopedic tapes market can be classified into two main types: rigid and flexible tapes. Rigid tapes, typically made from strong materials, provide maximum support and stability for injured joints or limbs, making them suitable for immobilization during recovery. In contrast, flexible tapes are designed for greater adaptability and comfort, allowing movement while still providing support. These tapes are commonly used for managing mild injuries, preventing strains, and aiding in rehabilitation, catering to varying patient needs and treatment protocols.</p></p>
