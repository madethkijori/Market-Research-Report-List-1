<p><ul><li>Linear Potentiometers</li><li>Rotary Potentiometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The potentiometers market is primarily categorized into linear and rotary types. Linear potentiometers feature a straight, sliding track for adjusting resistance and are commonly used in applications like audio equipment and sensors. In contrast, rotary potentiometers involve a circular motion to vary resistance, making them ideal for controls like volume knobs and tuning dials. Both types find application across diverse sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation, driven by the increasing demand for precise control in electronic systems.</p></p>
