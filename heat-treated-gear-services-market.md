<p><p>The heat treated gear services market encompasses various treatment processes that enhance the mechanical properties of gears. Normalizing heat treatment stabilizes the microstructure, improving toughness and strength. Quenching heat treatment involves rapid cooling to harden the material, enhancing wear resistance. Tempering heat treatment follows quenching to reduce brittleness while maintaining hardness. Annealing heat treatment softens materials to relieve stress and improve ductility. Each type serves specific applications, optimizing gear performance in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
