<p><strong>The Vehicle Accident Emergency Call System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Trigger</li><li>Automatic Trigger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Accident Emergency Call System market is divided into two main types: Manual Trigger and Automatic Trigger. Manual Trigger systems allow occupants to manually activate an emergency call in case of an accident, providing control to the user. In contrast, Automatic Trigger systems automatically detect an accident through sensors and immediately initiate a call for help, ensuring rapid response even if the occupants are incapacitated. Both systems aim to enhance emergency response efficiency and improve safety outcomes for vehicle occupants.</p></p>
