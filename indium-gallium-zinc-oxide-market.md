<p><p>The Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide (IGZO) market is primarily segmented into two types: crystalline IGZO and amorphous IGZO. Crystalline IGZO features a well-ordered atomic structure, providing superior electronic properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications such as advanced displays and flexible electronics. In contrast, amorphous IGZO lacks a defined crystalline structure, offering ease of fabrication and flexibility, often used in large-area electronics like televisions and mobile devices. Together, these types cater to diverse technological needs across various industries.</p></p>
