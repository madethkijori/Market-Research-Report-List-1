<p><p>Real estate brokerage software is available in two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed locally on an agency's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based software is hosted off-site by service providers, enabling easy access from anywhere, automatic updates, and lower initial costs. This flexibility and scalability make it an attractive option for modern brokerages seeking efficiency and collaboration in their operations.</p></p>
