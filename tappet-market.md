<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tappet market encompasses components used in internal combustion engines to manage valve movement. Flat tappets are traditional designs that directly contact the camshaft, suitable for various applications but prone to wear. Roller tappets, featuring a rolling element, minimize friction and enhance efficiency, extending engine life and performance. As engine technology evolves, the demand shifts towards roller tappets for better fuel efficiency and durability, with flat tappets still finding use in classic and cost-sensitive vehicles.</p></p>
