<p><p>The artificial skins market is divided into two primary types: epidermal skin material and dermal skin material. Epidermal skin materials mimic the outer layer of human skin, often used in wound healing and cosmetic applications. They provide a protective barrier and promote healing. Dermal skin materials replicate the inner layer, offering structural support and aiding in tissue regeneration. Both types are essential in medical procedures, enhancing recovery, and improving the quality of life for patients with severe skin injuries.</p></p>
