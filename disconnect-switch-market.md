<p><strong>The Disconnect Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused</li><li>Non-Fused</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disconnect switch market is generally categorized into two main types: fused and non-fused switches. Fused disconnect switches incorporate a fuse element that protects the circuit by interrupting the current in case of an overload or short circuit. This additional safety feature makes them suitable for applications requiring enhanced protection. Non-fused disconnect switches, on the other hand, lack this fuse component, offering a simpler design primarily for isolating electrical equipment. They are often used in lower-risk environments where fuses are deemed unnecessary.</p></p>
