<p><p>The LAN Transformers market is segmented into Power over Ethernet (PoE) and Non-PoE categories. PoE transformers integrate power supply capabilities into data transmission, allowing devices like IP cameras and VoIP phones to receive both electricity and data over a single cable, enhancing efficiency and reducing installation costs. In contrast, Non-PoE transformers are used in traditional setups where power and data are supplied separately. This segmentation reflects varying user needs and technological advancements in network infrastructure, influencing market growth and adoption.</p></p>
