<p><ul><li>External Blower</li><li>Internal Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The downdraft range hoods market is segmented into two main types based on blower configuration: external and internal blowers. External blowers are installed separately from the hood, typically on the roof or exterior wall, providing more powerful ventilation and quieter operation. Internal blowers, on the other hand, are built into the hood itself, offering a compact design suitable for smaller spaces but may be less powerful and noisier. Each type caters to different kitchen layouts and ventilation needs.</p></p>
