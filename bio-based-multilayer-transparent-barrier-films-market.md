<p><p>The bio-based multilayer transparent barrier films market comprises three primary types: extrusion coating, dispersion coating, and atomic layer deposition. Extrusion coating involves melting plastic and applying it as a continuous layer, while dispersion coating utilizes a liquid coating material that forms a film upon drying. Atomic layer deposition is a precise technique that deposits thin layers atom by atom, ensuring uniformity and performance. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to varying applications in sustainable packaging and protection.</p></p>
