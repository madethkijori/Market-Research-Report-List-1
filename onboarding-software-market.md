<p><p>Onboarding software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based onboarding software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, allowing for easy scalability, automatic updates, and remote access. This model is often preferred for its cost-effectiveness and flexibility. On-premises onboarding software, however, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. Each option caters to different organizational needs and IT capabilities.</p></p>
