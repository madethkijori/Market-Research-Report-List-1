<p><p>The self-priming pumps market primarily features two types: ZW Type and QW Type self-priming pumps. ZW Type pumps are designed for clear water applications, ideal for water supply and drainage, offering efficient priming and suction capabilities. In contrast, QW Type pumps handle wastewater and sludge, equipped to manage solids and abrasive materials. Both types are favored for their ability to draw fluid without needing manual priming, enhancing operational efficiency across various industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
