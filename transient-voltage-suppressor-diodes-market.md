<p><p>The Transient Voltage Suppressor Diodes (TVS) market is segmented into uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes predominantly clamp positive voltage transients and are commonly used in applications with unidirectional voltage spikes, offering efficient protection in sensitive electronics. Bi-polar TVS diodes, on the other hand, handle both positive and negative voltage transients, providing more versatile protection in circuits where bidirectional surge suppression is necessary. Both types are crucial for safeguarding electronic components from transient overvoltages.</p></p>
