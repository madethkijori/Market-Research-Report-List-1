<p><p>The thermally conductive PU adhesive market is divided into single-component and multi-component types. Single-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to heat or moisture, offering convenience and quick application. In contrast, multi-component adhesives require the mixing of two or more components before use, allowing for enhanced customization in terms of thermal conductivity and curing properties. Both types are crucial in electronics, automotive, and aerospace industries, where efficient heat management is essential for performance and reliability.</p></p>
