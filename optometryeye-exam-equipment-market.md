<p><p>The optometry and eye exam equipment market includes various specialized devices. Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides detailed cross-sectional images of the retina. Fundus cameras capture detailed images of the interior surface of the eye. Perimeters measure visual fields to assess peripheral vision. Topography maps the curvature of the cornea. Lensmeters determine the prescription of glasses. Chart projectors display visual acuity tests. Wavefront analyzers measure how light waves travel through the eye, aiding in customized vision correction solutions.</p></p>
