<p><ul><li>Francis Turbine Pumps</li><li>Volute Pumps</li><li>Vertical Turbine Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered pumps market includes various specialized types, such as Francis turbine pumps, volute pumps, and vertical turbine pumps. Francis turbine pumps are designed for high efficiency in hydropower applications, utilizing a runner shaped like a spiral to convert kinetic energy into mechanical energy. Volute pumps feature a spiral casing that evenly distributes fluid flow, making them ideal for water and wastewater applications. Vertical turbine pumps are compact and efficient, suitable for deep well applications, providing reliable performance in diverse industries.</p></p>
