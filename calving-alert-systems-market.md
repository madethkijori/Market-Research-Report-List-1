<p><p>Calving Alert Systems are categorized into three primary types: tail mounted, neck mounted, and other market options. Tail mounted systems attach to the cow’s tail, detecting calving signs through movement and pressure changes. Neck mounted systems are fitted around the cow’s neck and monitor activity levels and physiological signals. Other market options include ear tags or sensor-based systems, which can provide similar functionalities but may focus on different metrics or attachment methods, offering varied choices for farmers to ensure timely calving intervention.</p></p>
