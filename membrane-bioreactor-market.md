<p><p>The Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) market includes three primary types: Hollow Fiber, Flat Sheet, and Multi-Tubular membranes. Hollow Fiber membranes feature thin, cylindrical fibers with high surface area, optimized for compact designs and efficient filtration. Flat Sheet membranes offer a larger surface area in a simpler structure, facilitating easy maintenance and cleaning. Multi-Tubular membranes consist of multiple tubular modules, providing robust filtration and suitable for large-scale applications. Each type serves distinct needs in wastewater treatment and resource recovery.</p></p>
