<p><p>The Automotive Collision Avoidance System market comprises several key technologies: radar, lidar, cameras, and ultrasonic sensors. Radar systems use radio waves to detect obstacles and measure their distance and speed, offering reliable performance in various weather conditions. Lidar utilizes laser light to create detailed 3D maps of the environment, enhancing object detection accuracy. Cameras provide visual information for recognizing potential hazards; they are cost-effective but can be limited by lighting conditions. Ultrasonic sensors are used for short-range detection, aiding in low-speed maneuvers. Each technology contributes uniquely to enhancing vehicle safety and preventing collisions.</p></p>
