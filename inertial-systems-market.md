<p><strong>The Inertial Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attitude Heading Reference System (AHRS)</li><li>Inertial Positioning and Orientation Systems</li><li>Inertial Measurement Units (IMU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inertial systems market encompasses three primary types: Attitude Heading Reference Systems (AHRS), Inertial Positioning and Orientation Systems, and Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs). AHRS provides orientation data based on accelerometers and gyroscopes, essential for aviation and maritime applications. Inertial Positioning and Orientation Systems offer precise navigation capabilities by calculating position and movement. IMUs measure acceleration and rotational rates, serving as the foundational technology for various applications, including robotics, automotive systems, and mobile devices, enhancing navigation and stability.</p></p>
