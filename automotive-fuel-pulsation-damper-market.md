<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Pulsation Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Tube Type</li><li>Twin-Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel pulsation damper market consists of two main types: single-tube and twin-tube dampers. Single-tube dampers feature a single chamber to absorb fuel pressure fluctuations, providing a compact and cost-effective solution for reducing pressure spikes in fuel lines. In contrast, twin-tube dampers are designed with two interconnected chambers, offering enhanced damping performance by better managing pulsations and vibrations. Both types are crucial for enhancing fuel system efficiency and extending the lifespan of fuel components in vehicles.</p></p>
