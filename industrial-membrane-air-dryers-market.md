<p><p>Industrial membrane air dryers are utilized for efficient moisture removal from compressed air systems. The market is divided into porous and non-porous types. Porous type industrial membrane air dryers utilize microporous membranes to allow moisture to pass through while retaining air, making them effective for lower pressure applications. Conversely, non-porous type industrial membrane air dryers leverage dense membranes to separate moisture from air under higher pressure, making them suitable for more demanding industrial environments. Both types enhance operational efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
