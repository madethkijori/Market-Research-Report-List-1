<p><p>Tunable diode laser gas analyzers operate in two main market types: in-situ and extractive. In-situ analyzers measure gas concentrations directly within the process environment, offering real-time data without sampling, which minimizes the risk of contamination. Extractive analyzers, on the other hand, collect gas samples from the process and analyze them in a separate chamber, allowing for more controlled and precise measurements. Both types are crucial for monitoring emissions and ensuring regulatory compliance in various industries.</p></p>
