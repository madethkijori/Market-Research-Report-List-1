<p><p>The lateral pipe expansion joint market is categorized mainly into two types: pipe end type and flange type. The pipe end type features connections directly to the pipe ends, allowing for flexibility and movement absorption within pipelines. In contrast, the flange type incorporates flanged connections, providing a secure and stable interface for installation. Both types are designed to manage thermal expansion and vibrations, ensuring the integrity and longevity of piping systems in various industrial applications.</p></p>
