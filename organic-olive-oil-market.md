<p><p>The organic olive oil market consists of three key types: Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Olive Oil, and Olive Pomace Oil. Extra Virgin Olive Oil is the highest quality, characterized by low acidity and rich flavor, made from cold-pressed olives. Regular Olive Oil is a blend of virgin and refined olive oils, suitable for cooking. Olive Pomace Oil is derived from the by-products of olive oil extraction, using solvent extraction; it is often used in industrial cooking due to its lower quality.</p></p>
