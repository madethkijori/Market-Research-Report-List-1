<p><p>Noninvasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) is a revolutionary screening method used to assess the risk of certain genetic conditions in a developing fetus. By analyzing cell-free fetal DNA circulating in a pregnant woman's blood, NIPT can detect chromosomal abnormalities such as Down syndrome with high accuracy and low false-positive rates. This method has gained popularity due to its safety compared to invasive procedures like amniocentesis, which carry a risk of miscarriage.</p><p>The NIPT market is experiencing significant growth, largely driven by increasing awareness of prenatal health, advancements in DNA sequencing technology, and rising demand for early and reliable screening options. The growing prevalence of genetic disorders and an upsurge in maternal age contribute to this demand. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. Key trends influencing this growth include expanding applications of NIPT to detect additional genetic conditions, integration of advanced informatics tools, and the development of personalized healthcare solutions. Furthermore, collaborations between healthcare providers and genetic laboratories are enhancing accessibility and affordability, making NIPT a mainstream option for expectant parents around the globe.</p></p>
