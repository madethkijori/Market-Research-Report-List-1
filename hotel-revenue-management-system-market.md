<p><strong>The Hotel Revenue Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>On-Premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hotel Revenue Management System (RMS) market is divided into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises solutions. Cloud-Based RMS offers flexibility and remote accessibility, allowing hotels to manage reservations and pricing from anywhere with an internet connection. This model often involves subscription pricing and automatic updates. Conversely, On-Premises RMS requires installation on local servers, offering greater control over data but necessitating higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to optimize revenue through data-driven decision-making.</p></p>
