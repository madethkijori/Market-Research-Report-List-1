<p><p>Delimber market types refer to the various methods used to process recently cut trees for paper, lumber, or other forest products. Flail delimbers use rotating blades to remove branches efficiently, ensuring minimal damage to the trunk. Pull-through delimbers involve pulling the tree through a machine, which strips off limbs as it moves. Other methods may include stationary or portable delimbers, each tailored for specific operational needs in logging and forestry, enhancing productivity and resource optimization in wood processing.</p></p>
