<p><strong>The Bispecific Antibody Therapeutic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bearing An Fc Region</li><li>Lacking An Fc Region</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bispecific antibody therapeutic market is primarily categorized into two types based on the presence of an Fc region. Antibodies bearing an Fc region have enhanced stability, longer half-life, and can engage immune effector functions, making them suitable for a range of applications. In contrast, bispecific antibodies lacking an Fc region offer unique advantages such as reduced immunogenicity and more controlled pharmacokinetics, which can lead to improved specificity in targeting multiple antigens, enhancing therapeutic outcomes in various diseases.</p></p>
