<p><p>The ultrasound scalpel market includes two primary types: suction ultrasonic scalpels and cutting ultrasonic scalpels. Suction ultrasonic scalpels utilize high-frequency sound waves to cut tissue while simultaneously providing suction to remove blood and debris from the surgical site, enhancing visibility. Cutting ultrasonic scalpels, on the other hand, focus on precise tissue dissection with minimal thermal damage, allowing for cleaner cuts and quicker healing. Both types are essential in surgical procedures, offering improved control and efficiency compared to traditional methods.</p></p>
