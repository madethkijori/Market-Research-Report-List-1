<p><strong>The Semiconductor Pyrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Pyrometer</li><li>Infrared Pyrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor pyrometers market primarily includes optical and infrared pyrometers, both designed for non-contact temperature measurement. Optical pyrometers use visible light to detect temperature by measuring the intensity of emitted light from a heated object, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Infrared pyrometers, on the other hand, measure the infrared radiation emitted by an object, allowing for precise readings even at lower temperatures. Both types are critical in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring optimal process control and equipment efficiency.</p></p>
