<p><p>The commercial smart glass market encompasses three primary types: electrochromic, thermochromic, and photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass changes its tint through an electrical current, enhancing privacy and energy efficiency. Thermochromic glass adapts its tint based on temperature, automatically responding to heat for improved comfort and reduced energy costs. Photochromic glass adjusts its shading in response to light exposure, darkening in sunlight to protect interiors and occupants. Together, these technologies offer innovative solutions for energy conservation and enhanced user experience in various commercial applications.</p></p>
