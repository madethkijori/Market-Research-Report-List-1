<p><p>The aircraft materials market encompasses various types essential for aircraft manufacturing. Aluminum alloys are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for airframes. Steel alloys provide strength and durability, often used in landing gear and structural components. Titanium alloys offer a high strength-to-weight ratio, suitable for critical applications. Super alloys, characterized by their ability to withstand extreme temperatures, are used in engine components. Composites, combining materials for enhanced performance, are increasingly favored for their lightweight and high-strength characteristics in modern aircraft designs.</p></p>
