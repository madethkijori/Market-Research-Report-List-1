<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Engineered T Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumor Infiltrating Lymphocytes</li><li>T Cell Receptor {TCR}</li><li>Chimeric Antigen Receptor(CAR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered T cells market comprises three main types: Tumor Infiltrating Lymphocytes (TILs), T Cell Receptors (TCRs), and Chimeric Antigen Receptors (CARs). TILs are derived from the tumor environment and are expanded ex vivo to target cancer cells. TCRs are engineered to recognize specific tumor antigens presented by the major histocompatibility complex (MHC) on cancer cells. CARs are modified T cells that express synthetic receptors targeting specific antigens on tumor cells, enhancing their ability to destroy cancer.</p></p>
