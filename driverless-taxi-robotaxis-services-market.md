<p><p>Driverless taxi services, commonly known as robotaxis, are categorized primarily into Level 4 (L4) and Level 5 (L5) markets based on automation capabilities. L4 robotaxis can operate autonomously in specific conditions or geofenced areas, requiring minimal human intervention. In contrast, L5 robotaxis represent full autonomy without any human involvement, functioning seamlessly in all environments and situations. As technology advances, L5 services promise a transformative shift in urban mobility, enhancing accessibility, reducing congestion, and lowering transportation costs.</p></p>
