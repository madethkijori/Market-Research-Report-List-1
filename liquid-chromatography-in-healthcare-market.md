<p><ul><li>Ion-Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Partition Chromatography</li><li>Size-Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Supercritical Fluid Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid chromatography in healthcare is essential for analyzing biomolecules and pharmaceuticals. Ion-exchange chromatography separates charged particles, facilitating protein purification. Partition chromatography relies on differences in solubility, useful for separating organic compounds. Size-exclusion chromatography separates molecules based on size, ideal for purifying proteins and polysaccharides. Supercritical fluid chromatography uses supercritical fluids to enhance separation efficiency and speed, making it effective for various analytical applications. Together, these methods support drug development, quality control, and biomarker discovery in healthcare.</p></p>
