<p><ul><li>Aerial Tramways</li><li>Chairlifts</li><li>Gondola</li><li>Surface Lift</li><li>Material Ropeways</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable car and ropeways market encompasses various types designed for passenger transport and material handling. Aerial tramways consist of two terminals connected by a suspended cable, ideal for steep terrains. Chairlifts feature open chairs on a moving cable, primarily used in ski resorts. Gondolas are enclosed cabins, offering comfort and panoramic views. Surface lifts, like T-bars, help skiers ascend slopes. Material ropeways transport goods over difficult terrains, serving industries such as mining and construction, enhancing logistics efficiency.</p></p>
