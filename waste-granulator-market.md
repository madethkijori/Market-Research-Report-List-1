<p><ul><li>Beside-the-press Granulators</li><li>Central Granulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Waste Granulator market consists of various types, primarily including beside-the-press granulators and central granulators. Beside-the-press granulators are compact units positioned next to molding machines, enabling immediate granulation of waste material for efficient recycling. In contrast, central granulators are larger, centralized systems designed to handle higher volumes of waste from multiple production lines, ensuring a more streamlined and efficient granulation process. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing recycling efficiency and reducing waste in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
