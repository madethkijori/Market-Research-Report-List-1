Ethylene glycol is the most common coolant due to its effective heat transfer properties and low freezing point, though it is hazardous. Propylene glycol is a safer alternative, suitable for environments needing non-toxic options. Glycerol, derived from renewable sources, is less common but environmentally friendly, offering biodegradable properties. Each type caters to different safety and performance needs.</p></p>
